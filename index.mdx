COI Client is a client-side Minecraft Fabric mod that connects you to servers running the Circle of Imagination plugin. It delivers a rich in-game experience — bind up to 6 abilities to hotkeys, spin up an interactive ability wheel, watch cooldown timers on a configurable HUD, and see server-triggered visual effects render directly on your screen.

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Fabric and dependencies">
    Download [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) and Fabric API for Minecraft 1.21.1+, then drop both JARs into your `mods/` folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add COI Client">
    Grab the latest `coi-client-<version>.jar` from the [GitHub releases](https://github.com/ikeepcalm/coi-client/releases) page and place it in `mods/`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join a COI server">
    Launch Minecraft with the Fabric profile. When you join a Circle of Imagination server, COI Client automatically requests your available abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bind your abilities">
    Press **K** to open the Ability Binding screen. Select abilities for each slot and save. Use **Z–N** to activate them or **G** to open the Ability Wheel.
  </Step>
</Steps>
